The Author

The New God Flow is Michael Caswell's first book.

Born in the North of England and raised in the Cayman Islands, Michael now lives in Vancouver, BC. For the past 16+ years he has been in the photo pit shooting for SCENE IN THE DARK, his concert photography project, watching artists channel their art night after night.

He is also the founder of Shadow Caster Studios, an AI-driven creative holding company building AI-powered tools across operations management, photography, music software, and creative tooling, all in service of empowering independent artists. Through the studio he also builds websites for businesses.

His background spans eleven years as a video game developer and work in AV and Operations Management. He works with the Three Links charity at the Odd Fellows building, and in 2017 founded the Identity Lens Project, a human rights platform for activists and anyone who wants to share their story of identity.

His focus is now on music production, helping artists channel their music from the source, as he has done himself. Michael gives all credit to God for everything he has done in his life, and everything he will do in the future, Inshallah (God willing).
